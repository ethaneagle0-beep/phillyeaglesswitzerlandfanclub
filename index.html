Our story

From Philly Sundays
to Swiss Sundays.

We're a family who fell for the Eagles while living in Switzerland — chasing a stream at odd hours, doing time-zone math for kickoff, learning the chants a season at a time. Somewhere between the snap counts and the early alarms, we figured we couldn't be the only ones doing this alone. So we started a club: a place for any Eagles fan in Switzerland, Swiss, American, or somewhere in between, to find each other, watch together, and make a few thousand miles feel a little smaller on game day.

Go Birds. Wherever you're watching from.
